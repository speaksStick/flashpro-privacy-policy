About Flash Pro

Flash Pro is a powerful flashlight app with features like SOS, strobe, Morse code signaling, shake to toggle, and more.

Background Limitations

Due to Android system power-saving policies, features like Shake to Switch Torch On/Off may not work reliably when the app has been inactive or running in the background for a long time. Opening the app restores full functionality.
