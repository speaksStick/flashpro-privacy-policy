About Flash Pro

Flash Pro is a powerful and easy-to-use flashlight application designed to provide advanced lighting features such as SOS, strobe, Morse code signaling, shake-to-toggle, pocket protection, and customizable themes.

Important Notes & Limitations

Background Behavior:
Due to Android system power-saving and background execution limits, certain features like shake-based torch control may not function reliably if the app has been inactive or running in the background for an extended period. Opening the app restores full functionality.

Pocket Mode:
Pocket Mode is designed to turn off the flashlight when the device is placed face-down or remains stationary. Detection accuracy may vary depending on device sensors, orientation, and environment.

These behaviors are influenced by device hardware and operating system restrictions and may vary across different Android devices.
