Privacy Policy for FlashPro: SOS & Strobe Light

Effective Date: 1 January 2026

FlashPro (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the FlashPro mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Sensors: FlashPro accesses device motion sensors such as the accelerometer to enable features like Shake to Toggle and Pocket Mode. This sensor data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted externally.
  • Flashlight Access: The app uses your device’s camera flash to provide flashlight, SOS, strobe, and Morse features. FlashPro does not access camera images, videos, or media.
  • Advertising Data: FlashPro uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymous identifiers (such as advertising ID, device information, or interaction data) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate core flashlight features including SOS, strobe, Morse code, timers, and themes.
  • To enable motion-based features such as Shake mode and Pocket mode.
  • To display advertisements that help support the app.
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

FlashPro does not store personal data on external servers. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect and process data independently as governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

FlashPro may use third-party services including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

These services may collect information as required to function properly. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.

5. User Control

  • You can enable or disable Shake mode, Pocket mode, and notifications within the app.
  • You can limit ad personalization through your device’s advertising settings.
  • You may uninstall the app at any time to stop all data access.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user privacy. FlashPro does not collect sensitive personal information.

7. Children’s Privacy

FlashPro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
